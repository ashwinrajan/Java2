Subscribing/Unsubscribing to the RMI-USERS List


To subscribe:
  • Send email to: listserv@java.sun.com
  • In the body of the message, type: subscribe RMI-USERS

    After subscribing, you can choose to receive all of the RMI-USERS daily email batched into just a few email messages:

    • Send email to: listserv@java.sun.com
    • In the body of the message, type: set RMI-USERS digest

    If you decide at a later time that you'd rather receive all of the email messages individually:

    • Send email to: listserv@java.sun.com
    • In the body of the message, type: set RMI-USERS nodigest
To unsubscribe:
  • Send email to: listserv@java.sun.com
  • In the body of the message, type: signoff RMI-USERS

If you'd like to check out the RMI-USERS email archives, they can be found here.

You may also want to take a look at the answers to Frequently Asked Questions about Java RMI and Object Serialization.
